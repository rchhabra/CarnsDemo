Article 1: Definitions

In these Conditions, except where the context otherwise requires or where it is otherwise expressly provided, the following expressions have the meanings respectively assigned to them, that is to say:

Additional Services

Any products and services purchased by You and which are provided by third party providers i.e. car hire, hotel accommodation.

Baggage

Your personal property accompanying You in connection with Your trip. Unless otherwise specified, it includes both Your Cabin Baggage and Your Hold Luggage.

Cabin Baggage

Equivalent to hand luggage and includes all personal items taken onboard by a Passenger and is any Baggage that is not Hold Luggage

Core Languages

English, French, German, Italian or Spanish.
