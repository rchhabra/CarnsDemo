If you are booking Economy Plus or Premium, the following restrictions apply:

  • All fare(s) shown include all taxes, fees and charges as applicable.
  • Refundable and cancellable (additional fees will be applicable)
  • Exclude checked baggage and sports equipment.
  • Depending on the invoicing method selected, an Optional Payment Charge will be added to the displayed price. Please note that the OPC is not refundable.
Check-in (hold) luggage:
  • Fare includes one piece of standard hold baggage, one piece of cabin baggage with a maximum size of 50x40x20cm and one small under seat bag (handbag/laptop).
  • On the next page you can add check-in (hold) luggage to your booking and purchase extra offers, and you’ll be shown the relevant charges.
  • Pre-booking your hold luggage online is much cheaper than paying at the airport, and much less hassle.
