The best spots for culture in Paris...

Le Lucernaire

This place is a cultural crossroad and you won't regret to visit it. It is located in a former factory, it has its own cobbled street with 3 theatres which are: Théâtre Noir, Théâtre Rouge and Paradis, also counts with three cinema screens, a bookshop, an exhibition space, a bar and a restaurant, enjoy it!

Guignol

If you want to be a child again you can't miss this. In most of Paris gardens, such as Parc Montsouris or Parc des Buttes Chaumont, exists performances that last 40 minutes and are low cost per person, so don't miss this chance.

Maison Européenne de la Photographie (MEP)

This is a place not only for photographers but every person that wants to delightful with the beauty of images. It has a permanent collection but also retraces world photography from Koudelka to Sieff and Cartier-Bresson to Salgado.

Shopping in Paris

The greatness of being in Paris is that gives you the chance to get unique things from designers. Here are some essential boutiques that capture the shine of this city.

  • Geek-chic: Lulu Berlu, Manga Story, Little Tokyo, Album and Maxxigames.
  • Concept stores: French Touche, SoWeAre, French Trotters, Pop Market and La Fausse Boutique.
  • Kids: Balouga, Libbellule et Coccinelle, Le Ciel est à Tout le Monde, Loulou Addict and Maison DoDuDinDon.
  • Shoes: Comme des Chaussures, Mi-Prix, Garrice and Annabel Winship.
  • Wine: La Cava des Papilles, Wine by One, La Cava de l'Insolite and Le Verre Volé.

A night out in Paris

There are many things to do in Paris each night, so we highly recommend you these activities:

  • Check out some live jazz at Au Duc des Lombards or give a whirl going to Atelier de Charonne.
  • Don't miss a big musical at the Théâtre du Châtelet where they always have the best in classic music and dance performances.
  • If you prefer some independent gigs and performances you must go to Point Ephémère, a great outdoor where you can find all kind of artistic exhibitions in cutting edge pop, rock, electro and hip-hop.
  • Party at La Bellevilloise which is a multidisciplinary centre full of acoustic sounds, cocktails and exhibitions.
  • And last but not least, see a film at Le Louxor, an Egyptian art deco cinema that promotes cultural and artistic educational projects… You can enjoy a glass of wine on the upstairs bar.
