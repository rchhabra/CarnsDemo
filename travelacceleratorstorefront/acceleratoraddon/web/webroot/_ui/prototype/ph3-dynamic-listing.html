Perfectly located only 5 minute walk from the tube station, yet situated on a quiet residential street for a restful night's sleep.

I've stayed in many parts of London, and this is my favourite location. When you've come back from your daytime hustle and bustle, it's lovely to spend some quiet time in the beautifully appointed ensuite rooms, or walk to the end of the block for coffee and a bite to eat.
