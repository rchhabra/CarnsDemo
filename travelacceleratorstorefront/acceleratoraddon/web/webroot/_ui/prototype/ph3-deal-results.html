Sumo Tournaments in Tokyo

Sumo Tournaments or Sumo Wrestling

The January Sumo Tournaments, or the January Basho, are one of the year's six Grand Tournaments of sumo wrestling. Held for fifteen days from the second to fourth Sundays in January at Ryogoku Kokugikan Sumo Hall, it culminates in the crowning of grand champions, or yokozuna.
