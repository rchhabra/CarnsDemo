All rooms at this NH hotel are air-conditioned and come with carpeted or parquet floors. Some rooms are complete with a balcony, and some feature a tea/coffee maker.

The restaurant serves Ligurian seafood dishes, modern cuisine, and traditional Italian recipes. It features 2 summer terraces with a charming view of the port and of Genoa's symbolic landmark, La Lanterna. Breakfast is buffet style.

The property is member of Preferred Hotels & Resorts.

This is our guests' favorite part of Genoa, according to independent reviews.
